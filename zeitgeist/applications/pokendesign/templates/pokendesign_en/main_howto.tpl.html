How to use Poken cards

How to get a card background on your Poken card in five easy steps:

1.


Find a nice looking card and click on it to see the details page. Cick on "download" to save the card design to your computer.

2.


Log into www.doyoupoken.com with your Poken account.

3.


Click on "Identities" in the main menu. On the identities page, choose the profile you want to add the background to.

4.


On the bottom right, click "Personalize Card Design".

5.


In the popup menu, choose the background image you downloaded in step 1. Click "Upload" and there you have it - your new Poken card with your own design.
